Introduction

TechTics is a fusion of two words: "Tech" from Technology and "Tics" from Robotics. Our tagline, "Where Technology Meets Robotics," reflects our commitment to bridging the gap between innovation and hands-on learning. Founded in 2019 at the GEARs 19 competition (PAF-KIET), TechTics began as a student-led startup with a vision to create a platform where young minds could learn, innovate, and compete in robotics. We started with a small batch of just eight students and have now flourished into a thriving community, with over 300 graduates in Robotics and STEM.

Our Vision

To introduce a centralized robotics curriculum for students from Grade 3 to Grade 12 in Pakistan, while providing them with research and development opportunities. This dual approach enables students to transform their innovative ideas into reality and contribute meaningfully to the field of robotics.

RoboFest 2024: A Year of Innovation

Building on the success of last year's event, RoboFest 2024 raised the bar for robotics and STEM education in Pakistan.

  • 700+ Young Innovators: Joined forces to showcase their skills and creativity.
  • 4 Exciting Categories: Robotics, Programming, STEM, and E-Gaming.
  • 16 Electrifying Competitions: Teams went head-to-head, showcasing their exceptional skills.
  • Winners: Habib Girls' School Official took home the coveted first prize, followed closely by Fajr Academy Official.

We extend our heartfelt gratitude to our valued collaborators, including Aga Khan School, for their unwavering support. Together, we're shaping a brighter future for Pakistan's young innovators.
