Research Timeline

2023

3-in-1 IoT-based Car

This innovative system integrates automated car wash, interior detailing, and protective coating into one solution. Using IoT sensors and microcontrollers like Arduino or Raspberry Pi, it detects car size and shape to optimize water and detergent usage. Users can schedule sessions remotely via a mobile app, enjoying convenience, efficiency, and customization while reducing waste and environmental impact.

2022

SMART DUSTBIN

Our smart dustbin concept revolutionizes waste management with an intelligent system that autonomously collects trash and responds to user requests via a mobile app. Equipped with sensors, it automatically opens the lid for hands-free disposal and monitors fill levels, sending real-time updates to users' phones. Leveraging IoT integration and Bluetooth connectivity, this system enhances hygiene, increases efficiency, and provides a convenient user experience.

2021
