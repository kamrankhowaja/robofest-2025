About RoboFest

RoboFest is TechticsClub's flagship annual event that brings together robotics enthusiasts, students, and industry professionals for a day of innovation, competition, and learning.

Now in its 5th year, RoboFest has grown to become one of the largest student-led robotics events in the region, featuring multiple competition categories, workshops, exhibitions, and networking opportunities.

Whether you're a seasoned robotics expert or just getting started, RoboFest offers something for everyone. Join us for an exciting celebration of technology and creativity!

Competition Categories

Line Following Challenge

Design and program a robot that can follow a predefined path with precision and speed. Navigate through increasingly difficult tracks with curves, intersections, and obstacles.

Sumo Bot Wrestling

Build a robot designed to push opponents out of the ring. Strategy, power, and maneuverability are key in this exciting head-to-head competition.

Maze Solver

Create an autonomous robot that can navigate through a complex maze, find the shortest path to the exit, and return to the starting point.

Open Innovation

Showcase your original robotics project addressing real-world problems. This category rewards creativity, technical excellence, and practical applications.
